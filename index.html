Frequently asked questions

  • Can I get more than 1 Features?
    Yes, You absolutely can! We offer more features for more invites!
  • Can I Boost the Server to get one?
    Yes, You absolutely can! We offer features per boost as well!
  • Can I pay in money for a discord bot?
    Yes, You absolutely can! We offer discord bots at a cheap starting rate of 3$!

    It depends on the number of features and the developer assigned, usually it only take 12-24 hours per bot but it may take longer time.
